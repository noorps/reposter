How it works

From saved message to posted: in seconds

No more opening each group, finding the composer, and pasting your ad manually. Reposter does the repetitive part.

1

Save your message variants

Write once. Save your service offer or weekly ad as a reusable variant and switch between them any time.

2

Pick your groups

Choose from your saved groups or search for niche communities: Indian families, African parents, local professionals, and more.

3

Hit Post

Reposter opens each group, finds the composer, and pre-fills your text. You review and click Post in each tab.
